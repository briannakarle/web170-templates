About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to reinvent intensity as it exists in the craft beer world using creative combinations of the highest quality ingredients.

What We Stand For

It is our mission to ensure that Bad Jimmy's Brewing Co. consistently offers the most innovative creations when it comes to craft beer. We love our beer. We want to share our love with you.

At Bad Jimmy's Brewing Co. we stand behind our belief in and love for what we do and create. We know that the quality of our product is and always will be a direct reflection of the high standards we are committed to and will be remembered by. Our mission is to reinvent intensity as it exists in the craft beer world. We will do this with creative combinations of the highest quality ingredients for which we see no substitute.

In a world where deviating from the norm is often discouraged and frowned upon, we want to break the tradition and offer an escape- where being different is not only encouraged but also celebrated. We believe that if being different is bad we don't want to be good. We feel that to be different is to be brave, to be bold, and to be true to one's character. We embrace different in our lives and in our beer. Good enough is not good enough. This perspective is at the heart of our brand and enjoyed in our beer.

The hard work, courage, and determined motivation to chase down our own dream has positively influenced the bold flavors of our high hopped ales. Developed over time and crafted with experience, our recipes offer a well-rounded balance to the boldness we prefer in beer. We are thrilled to share these delicious flavors with you. What we believe to be true in life, we also believe to be true for beer.

Staff

Bad Jimmy's Brewing Co. is made up of 5 beer lovers who met in Seattle, Washington. Individually, our love for craft beer has been intense as long as we can remember. Together we are committed to the love we share for the brand we created.

Sustainability

At Bad Jimmy's, we love the earth and our community! We are dedicated to implementing environmentally friendly policies. An extensive amount of our interior design, including our tables and bar, was done with reclaimed pallet wood. Every little step towards helping the environment adds up!

Community

We care. A lot. We love to put on events to raise money for a good cause, and do so frequently! Check out our events page to see what philanthropies we have coming up. If you are interested in us helping you with holding an event, or if you need donations, fill out our form on our contact page.
